# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-20

Durak Imperia is an online card game. This policy explains what data is used and why.

## Data We Process

Durak Imperia may process:

- Account id, username, nickname, and optional avatar
- Gameplay history, statistics, tournaments, rewards, and inventory
- Virtual currency balances and purchase records
- Device id and IP address for security, antibot detection, abuse prevention, and moderation
- Voice chat signaling metadata when players choose to use voice chat
- Crash, server, and diagnostic logs needed to operate the service

## Why We Use Data

We use data to:

- Run multiplayer games and tournaments
- Keep player progress and virtual inventory
- Prevent abuse, cheating, spam, and bot activity
- Provide optional purchases and rewarded video rewards
- Improve reliability and support users

## Ads

Durak Imperia uses rewarded video ads only. Forced interstitial, app open, and banner ads are disabled for the first release.

AdMob category controls must block gambling, betting, and social casino ads.

## Purchases

Purchases are used for in-game benefits such as Gold Coin, Premium, and cosmetic items. The game does not provide real-money gambling or cash-out.

## Children

The app should be configured in Play Console according to the final target age rating and local legal requirements.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be processed by infrastructure, payment, authentication, and ad providers only as needed to operate the app.
