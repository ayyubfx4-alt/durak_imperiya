Durak Imperia Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-20

Durak Imperia is an online card game. We use data only to operate the game, protect players, process optional purchases, and improve reliability.

Data We Process

  • Account id, username, nickname, and optional avatar.
  • Gameplay history, tournaments, rewards, inventory, and virtual currency balances.
  • Purchase records for Gold Coin, Premium, and donations.
  • Device id and IP address for antibot, security, abuse prevention, and moderation.
  • Voice chat signaling metadata when players choose to use voice chat.
  • Crash, server, and diagnostic logs needed to run the service.

Ads

Durak Imperia uses rewarded video ads only. Forced interstitial, app open, and banner ads are disabled for the first release. AdMob category controls must block gambling, betting, and social casino ads.

Purchases

Purchases are used for in-game benefits such as Gold Coin, Premium, and cosmetic items. The game does not provide real-money gambling or cash-out.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be processed by infrastructure, payment, authentication, and ad providers only as needed to operate the app.
